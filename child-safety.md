# Child Safety Standards – Velora

Velora is committed to ensuring a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

## Our Standards

* We strictly prohibit any content that involves or promotes child sexual abuse or exploitation.
* Users are not allowed to upload, share, or distribute harmful or illegal content.
* Any violation will result in immediate account suspension or permanent ban.

## Reporting & Moderation

* Users can report inappropriate content or behavior.
* Reported content is reviewed and appropriate action is taken promptly.
* We cooperate with law enforcement when required.

## User Responsibility

* Users are responsible for the content they share.
* Any misuse of the platform will lead to strict action.
